Exploring Madrid's Protected Buildings

Description of the situation:

In this application, you can explore an interactive map that displays protected buildings in the city of Madrid, Spain.

Information about Protected Buildings

Below, you will find information about some of the protected buildings represented on the map:

  • Royal Palace: The Royal Palace of Madrid is a stunning historic palace that serves as the official residence of the Spanish royal family. It is an architectural masterpiece and a symbol of Spanish monarchy.
  • Almudena Cathedral: The Almudena Cathedral is a beautiful neo-Gothic cathedral located near the Royal Palace. It is a prominent religious landmark in Madrid.
