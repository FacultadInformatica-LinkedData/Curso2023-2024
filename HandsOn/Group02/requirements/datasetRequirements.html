Requirements:

R1. Data are in one of the selected smart city domains:

Data showing energy consumption in Madrid municipal buildings.

R2. Data are available as a CSV file.

It is available as CSV and XSLX also.

R3. Data have an open license so they can be published.

Open license: authorizing the carrying out of reproduction, distribution, public communication or transformation activities with the data.

R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).

Dataset includes location such as neighborhood, district and coordinates of buildings.

R5. Documentation exists for the data (optional).

Documentation exists for the dataset, explaining structure and type of data included. The degree of satisfaction for this requirements is high.

R6. Data can come from multiple data sources (optional).

Data source is "Ayuntamiento de Madrid", taken from the advanced following of energy consumption in facilities of the Madrid City Council and its Autonomous Bodies.
